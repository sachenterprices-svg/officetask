Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the IPPBX hardware really free? ▼
Yes, under the BSNL scheme, the IPPBX hardware is provided at zero cost. There is no purchase price, lease, or EMI for the hardware unit itself.
Do I need an existing BSNL connection? ▼
Having an existing BSNL connection helps, but it is not mandatory. We can arrange a new BSNL PRI line or SIP trunk as part of the scheme application process.
What are the monthly charges? ▼
Monthly charges depend on the BSNL plan selected for your PRI line or SIP trunk connectivity. The IPPBX system itself does not have any separate monthly fee.
How long does installation take? ▼
Once the scheme is approved by BSNL, installation is typically completed within 3-5 business days depending on the size of your setup and cabling requirements.
Can I add more extensions later? ▼
Yes, the Sipon IPPBX system is fully scalable. You can add more extensions, departments, and even additional branches at any time.
Is this scheme available in my city? ▼
The scheme is available across India wherever BSNL has coverage. Contact us with your location details and we will confirm availability in your area.
